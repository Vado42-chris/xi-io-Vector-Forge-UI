### 1. **Wargame Methodology**

**The Process**:
1. Fire Teams scout (fast reconnaissance)
2. Multiple specialized teams test (edge cases, flows, integration, failure modes)
3. All findings aggregated
4. Critical issues fixed
5. Final plan created

**The Philosophy**: "Find every hole before writing code"
